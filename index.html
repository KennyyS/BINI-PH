BINI

Bini (stylized as BINI) is an eight-member Filipino girl group formed by ABS-CBN Star Hunt Academy, consisting of Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, Jhoanna, and Sheena. Their pre-debut single "Da Coconut Nut" was released on November 20, 2020. Bini officially debuted on June 11, 2021, with their single, "Born to Win," after two years of training under Star Hunt Academy.
