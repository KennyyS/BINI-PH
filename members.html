BINI Members

Maloi

Mary Loi Yves Kipte Ricalde (born May 27, 2002) better known as Maloi, is a Filipino rookie performer. She is the third eldest member and main vocal of the Filipino girl group BINI.


Her favourite colours are light pink, black, and white. Maloi's hobbies include watching k-dramas, playing guitar and ukulele. Her special talent is composing songs. She was voted as the "Rice President" by the SHA trainees because she eats the most out of all of them. She wasn't planning on auditioning because she was focused on her academics, but since her younger sister wanted to she went along with her and got through the auditions. Maloi's favourite SHA subject is singing. Her life motto is: "Life is short, so always choose to be happy."

Mikha

Mikhaela Janna Jimenea Lim (born November 8, 2003) better known as Mikha, is a Filipino rookie performer. She is the third youngest member, main rapper, lead dancer and visual of the Filipino Girl Group BINI.


She was born in Cebu City. She then moved to Sta. Rosa Laguna for 2 years then moved to Silang, Cavite for 6 years then San Juan for 1 year then QC from 2019 til the present for BINI. Mikha's family currently lives in Silang, Cavite and her brother in the US. Her favourite colours include red and black. She loves playing sports and sleeping as well as watching k-dramas and anime. Her special talent is flexibility. She learned was a cheer dancer for almost 2 years but claims to still not know how to dance when entering the training program. Her favourite SHA subject is singing. Her life motto is: "Dreams will only stay dreams unless you act upon it."

Aiah

Maraiah Queen Arceta (born January 27, 2001) better known as Aiah, is a Filipino rookie performer. She is the eldest member, main rapper & visual of the Filipino girl group BINI.


Aiah's favorite color is Black. Aiah's hobbies include swimming and drawing. Her special talent is modelling. She auditioned for Pinoy Big Brother but wasn't successful, instead her former casting manager suggested an audition for Darna's (a popular Filipino soap opera) role to gain exposure from StarHunt's managers. Luckily Starhunt looked through her auditions again and brought her to the training program of StarHunt Academy. Her favourite SHA subject is dancing. Her life motto is: "Keep your eyes on the stars and your feet on the ground."
